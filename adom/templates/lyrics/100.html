1. Songs of angels I would cherish, I must hear God's angels sing; Singing joyfully to welcome, Christ the Son of God the Lord; Saying we must be of courage, Joyful news they have for all now. Saying gladly to the whole world, Mankind's Savior has been born.

2 He is Christ our Lord and Master, He was born of David's line; Truly this is joyful, best news, For all that are lost in sin; Men and women of all races, Children and those that are aged, Must receive this Gift from Heaven, With a heart of faith in God.

3 Heaven's choir joined in the chorus, Of the Angel's joyful hymns; Laud and Praising God the Father, Praising God with joyful hearts; Saying, Glory to God on high, With goodwill and peace to all men. Peace on earth and on all nations, With God's favor on mankind.
