1. Sing with the tongue and heart and [soul, Sing to our gracious Lord. Sing of His love and wondrous works, And testify of Him.

2 Lord, you are source of all my life, Eternal God, you are. From you, we have all things we need, Good things we need in life.

3 Lord, we are nothing in this life, Nothing we have on earth. All that we have come from you Lord, You made them all for us.

4 Who made the heav'ns and made the clouds?] So majestic we see. Who sends the rain we need for life? That waters earth for food?
