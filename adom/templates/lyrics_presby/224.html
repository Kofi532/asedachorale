3 Crown Him the Lord of life Who triumphed oer the grave Who rose victorious to the strife For those He came to save His glories now we sing Who died and rose on high Who died eternal life to bring And lives that death may die

4 Crown Him the Lord of heavn One with the Father known One with the Spirit through Him givn From yonder glorious Throne To Thee be endless praise For Thou for us hast died Be Thou O Lord through endless days Adored and magnified
