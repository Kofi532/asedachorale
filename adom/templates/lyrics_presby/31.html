1. God, who made the earth, The air, the sky, the sea, Who gave the light its birth, Careth for me.

2 God, who made the grass, The flow'r, the fruit, the tree, The day and night to pass, Careth for me.

3 God, who made the sun, The moon, the stars, is He Who, when life's clouds come on, Careth for me.

4 God, who made all things, On earth, in air, in sea, Who if I lean on Him, Careth for me.

5 When in Heav'n's bright land I all His loved ones see, I'll sing with that blest band, Careth for me.

6 God who gave His Son, Jesus who died for me, Help me to lean on you, You'll care for me.
