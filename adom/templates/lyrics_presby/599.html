1. Eternal Ruler of the ceaseless round Of circling planets singing on their way Guide of the nations from the night profound Into the glory of the perfect day Rule in our hearts that we may ever be Guided and strengthened and upheld by Thee

2 We are of Thee the children of Thy love The brothers of Thy wellbeloved Son Descend O Holy Spirit like a dove Into our hearts that we may be as one As one with Thee to whom we ever tend As one with Him our Brother and our Friend

3 We would be one in hatred of all wrong One in our love of all things sweet and fair One with joy that breaketh into song One with the grief that trembleth into prayer One in power that makes the children free To follow truth and thus to follow Thee

4 O clothe us with Thy heavenly armour Lord Thy trusty shield Thy Sword of Love Divine Our inspiration be Thy constant word We ask no victories that are not Thine Give or withhold let pain or pleasure be Enough to know that we are serving Thee
